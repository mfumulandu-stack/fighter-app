FIGHTER — FIND YOUR OPPONENT

Fighter is the first combat sports matching app for German-speaking countries. Whether you're looking for a sparring partner, training partner, or opponent for an official bout — Fighter connects you with the right athletes near you.

HOW IT WORKS:
Create your combat sports profile with your style, weight class and fight record. Swipe through fighters in your area. When both of you like each other, you have a match — and can chat directly, arrange training sessions or send a Fight Request.

FEATURES:
• Smart Matching by fighting style, weight class and GPS location
• Direct chat with your matches
• Fight Request System — arrange sparring or competitions
• Gym directory with over 50 verified gyms in DACH region
• World rankings for amateurs and professionals
• Events — community sparrings and training sessions in your city
• Gym verification — show your membership
• Verified fight record with win rate and KO ratio
• Dark Mode

FIGHTING STYLES:
Boxing · Kickboxing · MMA · Muay Thai · BJJ · Grappling · Wrestling · Judo · Sambo · Taekwondo · Karate · Kung Fu

YOUR COMMUNITY:
Active fighters in Germany, Austria and Switzerland. New members joining daily from around the world.
